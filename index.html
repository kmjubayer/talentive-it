What is an IT Institute

An IT (Information Technology) institute is an educational institution that provides specialized training and education in computer technology, software development, networking, and related fields. IT institutes offer a variety of programs, including short courses, certificate programs, associate degrees, bachelor's degrees, and postgraduate degrees.
